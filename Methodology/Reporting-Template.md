# 📝 Reporting Template

---

## Recommended Structure / Structure recommandée

---

### 1. Executive Summary / Résumé exécutif

- Objective of the investigation
- Key findings (3–5 bullet points maximum)
- Conclusions and immediate recommendations

---

### 2. Scope & Methodology / Périmètre & Méthodologie

- Target(s) investigated
- Timeframe of the investigation
- Tools and sources used
- Legal and ethical framework applied
- Limitations and known gaps

---

### 3. Findings / Résultats

- Organized by theme or chronology — choose one and stay consistent
- Each finding supported by at least one cited source
- Screenshots and archives referenced as annexes
- Technical indicators (IPs, hashes, domains, wallet addresses) formatted in code blocks

---

### 4. Analysis / Analyse

- Connections and patterns identified across findings
- Contradictions or gaps explicitly noted
- Confidence level for each conclusion:

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| ✅ Confirmed | Verified by multiple independent sources |
| ⚠️ Probable | Supported by strong indicators — not independently corroborated |
| ❓ Unconfirmed | Single source or unverified — treat as lead only |

---

### 5. Conclusion

- Summary of key takeaways
- Recommended next steps or actions
- Limitations of the investigation
- What would increase confidence in unconfirmed findings

---

### 6. Sources & References

- All sources listed with full URLs and access dates
- Archived versions (Wayback Machine, archive.ph) where available
- Raw data files or exports referenced

---

## ⚠️ Reminders / Rappels

- Never include raw PII (Personally Identifiable Information) without necessity
- Always clearly distinguish facts from assessments and interpretations
- Date and sign every report
- Specify the confidence level for every non-trivial claim
- Never publish findings that could endanger a source or an individual
