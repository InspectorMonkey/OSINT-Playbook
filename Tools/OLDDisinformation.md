## 🔎 Methodology / Méthodologie

**EN**
1. Identify the narrative or claim to investigate
2. Trace the origin — who posted it first and when?
3. Analyze account behavior — creation date, posting frequency, follower ratio
4. Check for bot activity (Botometer) and coordinated inauthentic behavior
5. Map the spread of the narrative across platforms
6. Cross-reference with verified fact-checks (Google Fact Check Explorer)
7. Identify amplification networks — who is pushing this narrative and why?
8. Document the full information chain with archives

**FR**
1. Identifier le narratif ou la affirmation à investiguer
2. Tracer l'origine — qui l'a publié en premier et quand ?
3. Analyser le comportement des comptes — date de création, fréquence de publication, ratio followers
4. Vérifier l'activité de bots (Botometer) et les comportements inauthentiques coordonnés
5. Cartographier la propagation du narratif sur les plateformes
6. Recouper avec les fact-checks vérifiés (Google Fact Check Explorer)
7. Identifier les réseaux d'amplification — qui pousse ce narratif et pourquoi ?
8. Documenter l'intégralité de la chaîne informationnelle avec archives

## 💡 Indicators of Inauthentic Behavior / Indicateurs de comportement inauthentique

| Indicator | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Account age vs activity | New account with high volume posting |
| Follower/following ratio | Abnormally high following, few followers |
| Posting hours | Activity at unusual hours for claimed timezone |
| Content repetition | Same narrative pushed across multiple accounts |
| Profile picture | Stock photo or AI-generated face |
| Coordinated posting | Multiple accounts posting same content simultaneously |

## 💡 Tips & Good Practices / Conseils

- Always archive before engaging — content gets deleted fast when exposed
- Check account creation dates — spikes around key events are suspicious
- Reverse image search profile pictures — stolen photos are common in fake accounts
- Look for linguistic patterns — machine translation leaves telltale signs
- State-sponsored operations often leave infrastructure traces — check domains and hosting
- Correlation is not attribution — document carefully before concluding
